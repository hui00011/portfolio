Resume

I am studing Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College which start me out on the path. I love all aspects of design and the challenge of answering a new brief with creative thought and practical theory. Being able to deliver a project that answers the clients needs in a way they never could have imaged is what makes this industry so exciting for me.

When I'm not working, I enjoy simple pleasures including travelling, biking and hanging out with the kids.
